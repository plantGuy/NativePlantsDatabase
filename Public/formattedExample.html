Aquilegia canadensis
Wild Columbine
Image Goes here

Flowers attract bumblebees, butterflies, hummingbirds. Native Columbines host 12 species of native caterpillars.

  • Height
    1-2 Feet
  • Bloom Color
    Red and Yellow
  • Bloom Date
    Mar -- May
  • Soil
    Adaptable, rocky; PH moderate-base
  • Natural Habitat
    Forests, Outcrops

After an initial burst of blooms in the spring, it may produce occasional blooms in the summer. In the right conditions, will self-seed readily. Semi-evergreen
